VOD Review Guide

The best way of fixing your mistakes is watching your gameplay and seeing what you're doing right and wrong. VOD reviewing is the best way to do that. After every tournament and scrim session you should be vod reviewing your mistakes in replays. You can also vod review pros and learn a lot from what they do right and wrong.

Do VOD reviewing as much as you can! You can learn from your own mistakes and also learn what your doing well and be more consistent in that! You will also find out what you need to improve on.
