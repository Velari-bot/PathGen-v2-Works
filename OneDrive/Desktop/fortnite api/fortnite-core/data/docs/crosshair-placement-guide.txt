Crosshair Placement Guide

What is crosshair placement? What is GOOD vs BAD crosshair placement?

Good crosshair placement is when you move your mouse as little as you need to. Bad crosshair placement is where you move your crosshair around more. (Ex: A Big Circle instead of a Small circle only selecting the edge of the tiles)

Why is good crosshair placement important?

Good crosshair placement can help you get faster, cleaner and more precise edits. Good crosshair placement is needed if you want to be a good Fortnite player! Bad crosshair placement makes you miss edits, edit slowly and miss your shots.
